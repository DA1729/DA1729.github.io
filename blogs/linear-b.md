# Linear B

I am studying 'Linear B' today. The oldest Greek dialect, evolved from 'Linear A' which was the Minoan Civilization's main language. Now Linear A still remains un-deciphered, but Linear B was deciphered in the 1950s by Michael Ventris, who built upon the work by Alice Kober.

While it was not used by the Minoans, it was used by another ancient Greek civilization, the Mycenaeans. I am really interested in actually studying this language, also the stuff that we uncovered about the Mycenaeans and ancient Greece through the scripts.
