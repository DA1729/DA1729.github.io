# The Library of Babel

Yeah, so, I am at my office, although I hate calling it office, because everyone will picture, well, the traditional, conventional office everyone hates going to. Not to brag, I LOVE being here. I am free to come whenever I want, free to leave whenever I want, and there is this really nice party hall to hang out in with these BIG ASS cushions, like OMG those cushions. It is like having a really big and nice home. I just love coming here and frying my brain out working on all sorts of math and the thing I will not name. Again, not under an NDA, but I just do not like presenting my half assed work and probably wrong hypotheses. And without any restrictions, I just happily get myself here every day at 9 in the morning and leave at around 7 pm, well, towards the end I usually have a piano session, yeah they have this really nice buffet piano in the hall.

My brain is fried right now, and I cannot process any more math and the rest of the work, so I will be writing on the thing I initially intended to write on yesterday, but ended up writing about something completely, well not completely (remember, by "saying", everything is the same if you think deep enough :p), different. And that is the library of babel.

Most of you might have heard about it, the library with all possible books that can be written, and might be like, ok, so what, it contains every book, it will be a huge library and stuff like that. Do you not realize that what I have written so far in this blog, and up to this section, repeated 1000 times (idk how many pages it will take for it to fill a book) will be present in that library.

As a matter of fact, I have no idea how this blog is going to look once finished, BUT, it is already written, like a near infinite number of times, in that library.

There must be a book which contains this phrase on repeat: "ping pong bottle score", then there will be a book with the same phrase on repeat but with my name somewhere in between, then keep replacing that name with all the other names in the world, still a valid book. I can literally keep typing till the end of time about all the books that are present there. OMG, that is a fun thought experiment to keep me up at night.

I think a lot about this, like a LOT. I first got introduced to the concept when I read the novella "A Short Stay in Hell" by Steven L. Peck, like what, 6 years back? I do not really remember. Still the best book I have read so far, because of the impact that book has had on my mind. Not a motivational book, in fact it is quite a simple story to follow. And the only key thing that impacted me was it introducing me to the library of all books, but it has become one of what I call my "core thoughts", thoughts I can say for sure define who I am. Obviously that list is incomprehensible... wow, the irony... but yeah, I certainly know that this thought is up there somewhere.

This guy, I do not remember the name, made this website [Library of Babel](https://libraryofbabel.info/), and it has this thing which I actually found scary, the [search tool](https://libraryofbabel.info/search.cgi), go play with it if you have the time.

Ok, that is all I had to write.
